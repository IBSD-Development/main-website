About Us

  • Who we are

    Integrated Business System Development, or IBSD, is a student-run organization that specializes in small business consulting and custom business system development. Our organization was founded on the principle of providing transformative products and services to the local businesses that need it most. Organized in two branches, business consulting and system development, IBSD provides students the opportunity to work on real world projects and make an immediate impact on the businesses in their community. We give small businesses access to top business and tech talent capable of revitalizing their operation.

  • Entrepreneurial Spirit

    As a startup, we proactively utilize uncertainty, ambiguity and change as opportunities for growth, emphasizing speed of execution and result-orientedness. We consider each of our members to be a co-owner of the company, whether they are a part of our consulting team or a part of our development team.

  • Result Oriented

    We are result-oriented and focused. We set challenging, competitive goals, focus on our output, take ownership of our projects, constructively confront and solve problems and execute our projects with focus and precision.

  • Proactive Communication

    We make it a point to deliver within deadlines and service levels set by our customers. Communication is key to our business and our tool of trade.

  • Lateral Thinking and Problem Solving

    To maintain our innovative environment, we strive to foster creative thinking in the interest of organizational objectives. Our goal is the transformative improvement of our client’s processes through the use of strategically implemented technology.

  • Quality

    We pride ourselves in being a customer-focused company providing quality service. As a part of our endeavor to honor and meet customer requirements we achieve the highest standards of excellence by focusing on doing the right things. We do this through constant evaluation, measuring and reviewing and believe in constant improvement and growth.

  • Passion and Excellence

    The IBSD family always celebrates every success and milestone together and the spirit of teamwork is a part of the work culture. We are passionate about our work and strive to become the benchmark of excellence in consulting and software development.

  • Embrace Change

    As a part of a dynamic and ever expanding tech industry, we encourage our members to challenge assumptions and adapt new ways of doing business. We make every effort to produce results to meet our customer's needs and eagerly embrace change.

  • Make an impact

    A productive and challenging work environment is the key to our success. We challenge our members to innovate and think critically on every project. Our members see the immediate impact of their work on a real business, in real time.

Services We Offer

Management consulting

Our consulting team analyzes every aspect of a business, providing research, expertise, and insight to the organization.

Technology strategy consulting

Our consulting and development team collaborate to provide meaningful strategy suggestions around the organization’s current management systems and processes.

Custom business system development

Finally, our development team builds a custom management software system designed to leverage the core competencies and meet the goals of the client business.
